Fiber
	> glass/plastic rod
	relies on light signals
	no electromagnetic interference (EMI)

	Single mode fiber (smf)
		> single core
		carry beam distance (50mi)
		laser diode

	Multi mode fibre (mmf)
		> thicker core
		multiple rays shorter distance (500m)
		light-emitting diode


Straight Tip (ST)
	> multimode fiber
Square/Standard Connector (SC)
	> square faced
Local Connector (LC)
	> similar to SC
Media Terminal recommended jack (MTRJ)
	> two fiber prongs/cables
	one send/other receive


Copper cables
	> are subject to electromagnetic interference (EMI)
	made of smaller individual cabling twisted together
	twists help to cancel out EMI
 
Unshielded Twisted Pair (UTP)
	> no braided metal shield
Shielded Twisted Pair (STP)
	> braided metal shield
